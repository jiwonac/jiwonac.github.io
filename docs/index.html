About

Hello! I am Jiwon Chang, a computer science student at the university of Rochester. I research representative data selection and integration under professor Fatemeh Nargesian and PhD student Pranay Mundra.

Outside of computer science, I can be found waxing philosophical about topics ranging biology, technocriticism, and environmentalism. I like walking simulators, card games, science fiction, plant-based cooking and cycling.

This website is the central catalog of interesting things I've done, from personal coding projects to research papers, non-academic writings and the occassional blog posts. Like a more human CV. The simple raw HTML/CSS design that evokes early internet aesthetics is intentional.
