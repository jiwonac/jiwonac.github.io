Hi! I'm JIWON CHANG, a computer science PhD student. I research data management algorithms at the University of Rochester's Data Intelligence Lab with professor Fatemeh Nargesian.

My current research interest lies at the intersection of data management, AI/ML, and randomized algorithms. I aim to enable in-database machine learning at interactive speed through approximate (core)set query algorithms. Some topics of current interest include:

  • Coresets in databases. In particular, (submodular) coresets for (deep) learning, applied to relational data.
  • Data management for model improvement. Querying & processing algorithms to diagnose and fix underperformance and representation issues.
  • Approximate non-aggregate query answering. Approximate top-k, package, and clustering queries.
