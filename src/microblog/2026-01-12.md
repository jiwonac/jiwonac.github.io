The DBML workshop at ICDE has featured research at the intersection between databases and machine learning since 2022. We welcome researchers and practitioners working in data management for the ML lifecycle, as well as those with expertise in learned components in databases or ML for solving data management problems. More information at [https://dataintelligencecrew.github.io/dbml26/](https://dataintelligencecrew.github.io/dbml26/).
